Wi-Fly

Wifi groups management for the win. No more slow connection, when there is better available.

Something

Maybe you have problems with your Wi-Fi network at home, at work or anywhere else. Phone does not reconnect to another known network even when it has much better signal. Wi-Fly solves your problem forever. All you need to do is to pair those Wi-Fi networks inside the application and it will take care of the rest. Once stronger network appears it will force your phone to switch to it.
