Dimmer

Never let your phone blind you during the night again!

When making our own projects, we always aim to solve one of our own problems. Dimmer is a perfect example of that. The minimum brightness on almost any mobile phone is just too bright for a usage during the night when all other lights are off, and therefore we were often annoyed by being blinded by it. The result is easy to use and can be set to automatically dim your screen for each night.
